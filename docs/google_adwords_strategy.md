# Google AdWords Strategy: Sams Valuations

## Executive Summary
This strategy focuses on capturing high-intent B2B leads from legal, fiduciary, and financial professionals. Unlike standard residential real estate ads, we are targeting **"The Professional Order-Maker"** rather than the individual seller.

---

## 1. Primary Keyword Verticals

### A. High-Intent Core Services
*   `probate broker price opinion`
*   `court appointed receiver california`
*   `expert witness real estate valuation`
*   `retrospective property valuation for estate tax`
*   `BPO for probate court`

### B. Vertical-Specific (Attorney/Fiduciary)
*   `partition action real estate broker`
*   `bankruptcy trustee property valuation`
*   `divorce property division BPO`
*   `conservatorship real estate accounting support`

### C. Competitor Alternatives
*   `commercial appraiser alternatives` (Position BPO as faster/cost-effective)
*   `probate referee valuation support`

---

## 2. Negative Keyword Strategy (CRITICAL)
To avoid wasting budget on retail "house hunter" traffic, we must aggressively exclude:
*   `homes for sale`, `zillow`, `redfin`, `realtor near me`
*   `residential appraisal` (unless coupled with BPO)
*   `how to sell my house`
*   `mortgage rates`
*   `rental listings`

---

## 3. Ad Copy Framework

### Value Proposition: "The Defensible Alternative"
*   **Headline 1:** Professional BPOs for Attorneys
*   **Headline 2:** Fast, Defensible Valuations
*   **Headline 3:** Sams Valuations | B2B Broker
*   **Description:** Retrospective and current market valuations for Probate, Divorce, and Partition actions. 1-hour response. Court-compliant documentation.

### Value Proposition: "Court-Appointed Expertise"
*   **Headline 1:** Court Appointed Receiver - CA
*   **Headline 2:** Expert Witness Real Estate
*   **Description:** Specialized brokerage for complex legal matters. Serving Trustees and Counsel since 2004. Get a free consultation today.

---

## 4. Landing Page Optimization
*   **Primary Destination:** `https://samsvaluations.com/contact`
*   **Deep Links:**
    *   Ad: "Expert Witness" -> `/services#expert-witness-testimony`
    *   Ad: "Probate" -> `/services#broker-price-opinions-bpos`
*   **Lead Magnet:** "Download our sample Court-Compliant BPO Report" (to capture email).

---

## 5. Budget & Bidding
*   **Bidding Strategy:** Focus on **Maximize Conversions** or **Target CPA**.
*   **Initial Budget:** Start with a focused daily spend ($50-$100) on the "Expert Witness" and "Receivership" keywords, as these represent the highest-margin engagements.
*   **Geographic Targeting:** Entire state of California (Primary), with a focus on Los Angeles, San Francisco, and San Diego legal hubs.

---

## 6. Success Metrics (KPIs)
1.  **Form Submissions:** Primary goal.
2.  **Phone Calls:** Track via Google Call Forwarding.
3.  **Cost Per Lead (CPL):** Target $40-$70 for high-value legal leads.
4.  **Conversion Rate:** Target > 5% on specialized landing pages.
