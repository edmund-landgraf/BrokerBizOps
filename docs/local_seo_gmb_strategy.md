# Strategy: Local SEO & Google Business Profile (GMB)

For a specialized brokerage like **Sams Valuations**, local search dominance is the highest-ROI marketing channel. Attorneys and heirs often search for "Probate Broker [City]" or "BPO Services [Zip Code]."

## 1. GMB Optimization (Google Maps)
Your Google Business Profile is your "Digital Storefront."
- **Primary Category**: Real Estate Appraiser or Real Estate Agency.
- **Secondary Categories**: Legal Services, Commercial Real Estate Agency.
- **Service Area**: Define specific counties (e.g., Los Angeles, Orange, Riverside) rather than just "California."

### The "Review" Engine:
- **Attorney Testimonials**: One review from a well-known litigator is worth 50 from residential sellers. Ask for reviews that mention "Professionalism," "Court-Defensible," and "Timely."

---

## 2. On-Page Local SEO
- **Location Pages**: Create hidden or visible landing pages for each target city (e.g., `samsvaluations.com/probate-broker-beverly-hills`).
- **Keyword Clustering**:
    *   *Probate*: "Probate house sale," "Inherited property valuation."
    *   *Litigation*: "Expert witness real estate," "Partition action broker."
    *   *General*: "BPO report California," "Date of death valuation."

---

## 3. Schema Markup (Structured Data)
Add "LocalBusiness" and "ProfessionalService" schema to the website. This tells Google exactly who you are, what you do, and which legal verticals you serve.

---

## 4. Content Strategy (The "Authority" Blog)
Don't write about "Home Decor." Write about:
- *"How to sell a house in an IAEA independent administration."*
- *"Why the Probate Referee's value might be wrong (and how to fix it)."*
- *"The Receiver's role in a high-conflict divorce."*

---

## 5. Local Citations
Ensure your **NAP** (Name, Address, Phone) is identical across:
- State Bar directories (as a vendor).
- Yelp (Business section).
- Bing Places.
- Local Chambers of Commerce.
- Fiduciary association directories (PFAC).
