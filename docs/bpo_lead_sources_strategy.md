# Strategy: BPO Lead Sources & Scouting Workflow

This guide identifies the primary platforms where BPO (Broker Price Opinion) and REO (Real Estate Owned) work is distributed and outlines a strategy for agents to "scour" these sites for high-value opportunities.

## 1. Top BPO & REO Asset Management Platforms
Brokers must be registered as "Vendors" on these platforms to receive order notifications.

### Primary "Exchanges" (High Volume)
- **[Clear Capital](https://www.clearcapital.com)**: One of the largest providers for banks and lenders. Known for consistent volume.
- **[SingleSource Property Solutions](https://www.singlesourceproperty.com)**: Frequently posts BPOs, inspections, and REO listing opportunities.
- **[Solidifi](https://www.solidifi.com)**: Focuses heavily on high-quality, data-driven valuations for major financial institutions.
- **[LRES](https://www.lres.com)**: Specializes in default management and commercial/residential valuations.

### Asset Management Companies (AMCs)
- **[ServiceLink](https://www.svclnk.com)**: Fidelity National Financial's arm for valuations and default services.
- **[Altisource / Hubzu](https://www.altisource.com)**: Heavy focus on REO disposition and bulk BPO portfolios.

## 2. Government & GSE Portals
- **[HUD Homestore](https://www.hudhomestore.gov)**: Monitor for "Management & Marketing" (M&M) contractors in your specific region.
- **[Fannie Mae (HomePath)](https://www.homepath.fannieame.com)** & **[Freddie Mac (HomeSteps)](https://www.homesteps.com)**: Critical for identifying default trends and securing REO listings.

## 3. The "Scout & Secure" Strategy
To win the best work, agents should not wait for emails; they must be proactive.

### Step 1: Portal Monitoring
- **Dedicated Hardware**: Have a secondary monitor dedicated to "Vendor Portal" dashboards.
- **Instant Alerts**: Configure SMS and Email alerts for "Zip Code" specific matches (targeting high-value CA markets).
- **Auto-Refresh**: Use browser extensions to refresh order boards every 60 seconds during peak hours (8 AM - 11 AM PST).

### Step 2: Filtering for "Best Fit"
Agents should ignore low-fee ($40–$60) "Drive-by" BPOs unless they are bulk. Focus on:
- **Full Interior BPOs**: Higher fees and leads to potential listing appointments.
- **Litigation/Probate Cross-over**: Orders marked for "Estate" or "Legal" purposes often pay 2x–3x standard rates.
- **Commercial BPOs**: Significantly higher complexity and pay.

### Step 3: Outreach & Relationship Management
- **Vendor Managers**: Every AMC has a regional Vendor Manager. Agents should reach out directly via LinkedIn or Phone to introduce **Sams Valuations** as a "Fiduciary-Grade" specialist.
- **Scorecard Maintenance**: Platforms rank brokers based on "Turnaround Time" and "Revision Rate." Aim for <24hr delivery and 0% revision requests to stay at the top of the queue.
