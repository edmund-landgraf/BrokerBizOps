# SOP: Fiduciary BPO Standards & Workflow

This Standard Operating Procedure ensures that every **Sams Valuations** report is court-defensible and meets the rigorous standards of probate attorneys, trustees, and financial institutions.

## 1. Pre-Inspection Research
- **Title Search**: Verify ownership and check for active liens or Lis Pendens that may affect valuation.
- **Zoning Check**: Confirm if the property has non-conforming use or ADU potential (massively affects probate value in CA).
- **Public Record vs. Reality**: Note discrepancies in square footage (GLA) between tax records and actual structure.

## 2. On-Site Inspection (Fiduciary Focus)
- **Deferred Maintenance**: Document every issue (roof, foundation, HVAC) with high-res photos. Fiduciary clients need "As-Is" values for estate settlement.
- **Property Preservation Status**: Note if the property is vacant, secured, or requires immediate stabilization (trash out, lock change).
- **Hostile Occupant Protocol**: If a family member is refusing entry, document the attempt and complete an "Exterior-Only" BPO with a formal disclosure.

## 3. Comparable Selection (The Rule of 3+3)
Every report should ideally include:
- **3 Sold Comparables**: Closed within the last 6 months (ideally 90 days).
- **3 Active/Pending Listings**: To show the current "competition" and market trajectory.
- **Proximity**: Comps must be within 1 mile (Urban) or 5 miles (Rural).

## 4. Adjustment Logic
- **GLA Adjustments**: Use a conservative price-per-foot factor (e.g., $50–$100/sqft) rather than the full market rate.
- **Condition Adjustments**: Be aggressive with deductions for probate properties in "original" condition.
- **Concessions**: Deduct for seller-paid closing costs in comp sales.

## 5. Final Reconciliation
- **Probate Referees**: Compare your findings with the Probate Referee's DE-160 (if available). If your BPO is significantly lower, provide a "Rebuttal Analysis" highlighting specific defects the referee missed.
- **Liquidation vs. Market**: Always provide both values. Attorneys need to know what it will sell for *today* vs. after a full marketing cycle.

---

*Fiduciary Duty Reminder: Our loyalty is to the Estate/Trust. All valuations must be neutral, data-driven, and devoid of "agent optimism".*
