# Rebrand Walkthrough: Sams Valuations

I have completed the full rebranding from **Meridian Valuations** to **Sams Valuations**. This update includes a new visual identity centered around the gold Medusa logo and a matching high-end color palette.

## 1. Visual Identity & Logo
- **New Logo Integration**: The `sams.png` Medusa logo has been integrated into both the **Navbar** and **Footer**.
- **Gold Theme**: I updated the Tailwind configuration to replace the previous orange brand colors with a sophisticated **gold/amber palette** (`#c5a059`).
- **Shadows & Accents**: All interactive elements (buttons, cards) now use `shadow-gold` instead of `shadow-orange`, creating a cohesive luxury look that complements the metallic tones of the logo.

## 2. Component Updates
- **Navbar**: The brand name is now "Sams Valuations" with the Medusa head prominently displayed.
- **Footer**: Updated the brand section, copyright notice, and social/contact links.
- **Hero Section**: Refined the background glow effects and grid overlays from orange to gold.

## 3. Content & Metadata
- **SEO**: Updated `index.html` title and meta descriptions for search engine optimization under the new brand name.
- **Social Proof**: All testimonials have been updated to reflect the rebranding, ensuring consistency in the company's "voice".
- **Contact Info**: Updated the official company description and contact email (`valuations@samsvaluations.com`).

## 4. Verification
- Ran a codebase-wide search to confirm that all 15+ instances of "Meridian" have been successfully replaced.
- Verified that the new gold theme maintains high contrast and readability against the premium dark backgrounds.

The site is now fully transitioned to the **Sams Valuations** brand!
