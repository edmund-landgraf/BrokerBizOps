# Industry Standard Rates & Fee Comparison

## 1. Valuation & Pricing Services

### A. Residential Broker Price Opinions (BPO)
*   **Rate Range**: $50 – $150
*   **Deliverable**: Comparative Market Analysis (CMA) or standardized BPO form.
*   **Nuance**: Fees are typically split between interior inspections (higher) and drive-by assessments (lower).
*   **Market Context**: Usually ordered by loan servicers for portfolio monitoring or pre-foreclosure analysis.

### B. Commercial Broker Price Opinions (BPO)
*   **Rate Range**: $250 – $500
*   **Deliverable**: In-depth financial analysis including income capitalization and market absorption data.
*   **Nuance**: Complex assets (multi-tenant retail, industrial parks) often command $1,000+ per report.
*   **Market Context**: Used by regional banks and private lenders for asset management and valuation refreshes.

### C. Retrospective Valuations
*   **Rate Range**: $250 – $600
*   **Deliverable**: Date-of-death or historical market value report.
*   **Nuance**: Requires extensive historical data verification to satisfy IRS or probate court standards.

## 2. Legal & Court-Appointed Services

### A. Expert Witness Testimony
*   **Hourly Rate**: $300 – $600
*   **Retainer**: Typically $5,000 – $20,000 upfront (Evergreen).
*   **Nuance**: Deposition and Trial testimony are often billed at a "premium" hourly rate (e.g., $500/hr) compared to case review.
*   **Takeaway for Sams**: High-margin vertical that relies heavily on the broker's specific CV and deposition history.

### B. Court-Appointed Receivership
*   **Hourly Rate**: $250 – $450
*   **Authority**: Governed by California Rules of Court (CRC 3.1183).
*   **Nuance**: Fees are paid out of the estate assets and must be approved by a judge via a formal fee application.

### C. Partition Referee
*   **Hourly Rate**: $300 – $450
*   **Total Case Cost**: Frequently ranges from $14,000 to $25,000.
*   **Nuance**: Involves neutral oversight of court-ordered property sales to resolve co-owner disputes.
