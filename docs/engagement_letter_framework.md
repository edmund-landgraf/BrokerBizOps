# Framework: Engagement Letters (Complex Services)

Since **Expert Witness**, **Receivership**, and **Partition Referee** services are excluded from the "One-Click Checkout," they require a formal Engagement Letter. This document outlines the required clauses and fee structures for these high-stakes B2B contracts.

## 1. Expert Witness Retainer
For litigation support, never start work without a signed retainer.

### Key Clauses:
- **Retainer Amount**: Typically $2,500 – $5,000 upfront.
- **Hourly Rate**: Specify rates for Research ($250/hr), Deposition ($400/hr, 4hr min), and Trial ($500/hr, full day min).
- **Evergreen Clause**: The retainer must be replenished once it falls below $500.
- **Work Product Ownership**: Clarify that the expert's opinion is independent and not "bought" by the hiring counsel.

---

## 2. Court-Appointed Receiver / Partition Referee
These appointments are made via **Court Order**, but an engagement memorandum is still required for the parties involved.

### Key Clauses:
- **Scope of Authority**: Reference the specific court order (e.g., "Power to secure, list, and sell the subject property").
- **Neutrality**: Explicitly state that the broker acts as an arm of the court, not an agent for either spouse/heir.
- **Reporting**: Weekly or monthly status reports to the court and counsel.
- **Commission**: Standard 5–6%, subject to court approval at the close of escrow.

---

## 3. The Digital Bridge (App Integration)
When a lead is captured for these services:
1. **Auto-Response**: Send a "Welcome & Credentials" packet (CV, Case Studies).
2. **Scheduling**: Link to a discovery call to discuss the case specifics.
3. **DocuSign Integration**: Send the specific Engagement Letter template based on the matter type.

---

## 4. Required Credentials Folder
Always keep a "Ready-to-Send" folder containing:
- **Broker License** (Current).
- **Curriculum Vitae (CV)**: Highlighting deposition history and fiduciary appointments.
- **Fee Schedule PDF**: For attorney file-opening purposes.
- **E&O Insurance Certificate**: Highlighting "Expert Witness" coverage.
